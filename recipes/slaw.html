Sweet Restaurant Slaw


Description

This sweet coleslaw recipe tastes like the slaw served at popular fried chicken or
fish restaurants.It's excellent with burgers or on top of BBQ pork sandwiches, too!

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) bag coleslaw mix
  • 2 tablespoons diced onion
  • ⅔ cup creamy salad dressing (such as Miracle Whip)
  • ½ cup white sugar
  • 3 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 tablespoon white vinegar
  • ½ teaspoon poppy seeds
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
